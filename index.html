GITADORA GALAXY WAVE DELTA Drummania Settings Generator (unofficial)

Set the shutter top as the judgment line to disable delay.

Explanation:
This tool calculates settings to align the SHUTTER (OUT) top as the judgment line, ensuring stable note display at 60Hz. HI-SPEED is interpolated based on BPM (e.g., BPM 78: x8.5, BPM 180: x3.75, BPM 400: x1.5). Changing BPM adjusts HI-SPEED, Note Display Adjust, Judgment Line Position, and SHUTTER (IN) to maintain the green number. Changing the green number or HI-SPEED adjusts other parameters while keeping BPM fixed. Non-editable fields are grayed out.

Caution:
- Konaste HI-SPEED adjusts in 0.5 units.
- Assumes GITADORA GALAXY WAVE DELTA's initial monitor delay is 9ms.
- Sound depth varies by song; use headphones for zero audio delay.
- HI-SPEED x3.75 at BPM 180 is the limit for stable notes.
