GITADORA GALAXY WAVE DELTA Drummania Settings Generator (unofficial)

Set the shutter top as the judgment line to disable delay.

This tool calculates optimal settings for GITADORA GALAXY WAVE DELTA Drummania, fixing the SHUTTER (OUT) top as the judgment line. Adjust BPM, HI-SPEED, or Green Number to update settings. The Green Number mimics IIDX's timing adjustment. Refresh rate is fixed at 60Hz, and monitor delay at 9ms.

Notes:
- Konaste's HI-SPEED adjusts in 0.5 units.
- Assumes initial GITADORA GALAXY WAVE DELTA monitors have 9ms delay.
- Sound balance varies by song; use headphones for no audio delay.
- HI-SPEED x3.75 at BPM 180 is the limit for stable note display.
