GITADORA GALAXY WAVE DELTA Drummania Settings Generator (unofficial)

Set the shutter top as the judgment line to disable delay.

This tool generates optimal Drummania settings for GITADORA GALAXY WAVE DELTA, fixing the SHUTTER (OUT) top as the judgment line to eliminate delay. Adjust BPM, Green Number, or HI-SPEED to calculate settings. The green number mimics Beatmania IIDX timing, ensuring notes align visually with a 9ms monitor delay at 60Hz. HI-SPEED is interpolated between BPM 78 (x8.5) and BPM 400 (x1.5), with BPM 180 at x3.75.

Notes:
※ Konaste HI-SPEED adjusts in 0.5 units.
※ The initial monitor is assumed to have a 9ms delay.
※ Sound depth varies by song; use headphones for zero audio delay.
※ HI-SPEED x3.75 at BPM 180 is the limit for stable notes.
